About Abbaya Tahini

Nestled in the heart of Coimbra District, Central Portugal, Abbaya Tahini brings you the authentic taste of Ethiopia’s finest Humera sesame seeds, sourced directly from small family farms in the Tigray region. Our tahini is crafted with time-honored techniques, blending tradition with care to deliver a luxuriously creamy texture and a rich, nutty flavor. Inspired by our love for quality ingredients and the vibrant culture of Coimbra, we create tahini that’s perfect for elevating any dish—from savory dips to sweet treats. Abbaya Tahini embodies the essence of heritage and craftsmanship in every jar.

Delicious Ways to Use Abbaya Tahini

From savory to sweet, discover recipe inspiration for every occasion!

Classic Creamy Hummus

Ingredients: 1 can chickpeas, 3 tbsp Abbaya Tahini, 2 cloves garlic, juice of 1 lemon, olive oil, salt, paprika.

Method: Blend chickpeas, tahini, garlic, and lemon juice until smooth. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle paprika. Serve with warm pita!

Lemon Tahini Dressing

Ingredients: 4 tbsp Abbaya Tahini, juice of 2 lemons, 1 clove garlic (minced), water, salt to taste.

Method: Whisk tahini with lemon juice and garlic. Add water gradually until desired consistency. Perfect for salads and grain bowls!

Tahini Honey Cookies

Ingredients: 1 cup Abbaya Tahini, ½ cup honey, 1 egg, 1 tsp vanilla, pinch of salt, sesame seeds for topping.

Method: Mix all ingredients, roll into balls, flatten slightly, and top with sesame seeds. Bake at 180°C for 10-12 minutes. Enjoy!
