Tahini and Heart Health

Sesame seeds contain powerful antioxidants known as lignans, including sesamin and sesamol. Research links these compounds to:

  • Reduced oxidative stress
  • Support for healthy cholesterol levels
  • Improved cardiovascular health

Using pure sesame tahini as an alternative to highly processed spreads can support heart health when enjoyed regularly as part of a whole-food diet.
